Bootstrap3 Design1 [51 Twitter Bootstrap 3 Design 1 - Create About Us Page] Concept:

This lesson => Create suggested Design of {about us} inner page in this website template

{About us} Inner page concept :
=> Inner page has custome contents and general information about the company / Fundation the owner of this website

=> Main Contents of {about} inner pages [in order]:

  • {Header} => The title of About inner page
  • {Nav} => The Navbar of all inner pages and main page
  • {Breadcrumb} => The Website Map Navigator of all inner pages inside the website
  • {About us Intro} => Contains the follwokng:
    1. Heading of the inner page
    2. Paragraph of a little of company or a slogan
    3. Image related to the Group or the company
  • {About us-features} => Main featrues of the our comapny => and may contents the folowing:
    => A Container of A Row Div with Several Columns , a Column for each feature
  • {Aboutus CEO} => A word of the CEO of the Company or Foundation => and may contents the following
    1. A container of A Row with serveral columns , [One column for the CEO of the company] & [Other for the word of the CEO] & [contact us button]
